Privacy

Browsing cards and building decks needs no account and collects nothing about you. You sign in only to save decks to your account and to play online, and even then we keep as little as the feature allows.

What we store when you sign in

Sign-in is handled by Google. We never see or store your Google password. From the verified sign-in we keep your display name and a stable Google account identifier (so the same account signs in to the same profile).

We do not store your email address in readable form. It is kept only as a one-way keyed hash, which lets us catch duplicate accounts and enforce bans without ever holding the address itself — a database breach exposes no email addresses.

Your decks and sessions

Decks you save are stored against your account. A sign-in cookie keeps you logged in; it is a random token, set HttpOnly and Secure, used for nothing but your session. We use no third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time. Deletion erases your profile, your saved decks, and your active sessions. If the account was banned, we retain only the keyed hashes described above so the ban cannot be evaded by deleting and re-registering; nothing else is kept.
